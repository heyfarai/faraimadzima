I'm Farai Madzima, an interaction designer, conference organiser, and speaker living in Ottawa, Canada.

Interaction design

I work as a UX Lead at Shopify in Ottawa.

In my last role, I led the design team at the Standard Bank Group in South Africa. We worked on large, complex, design challenges to try make money matters simple to understand, and simple to manage for everyone.

Organising Conferences

I co-organise two conferences in South Africa: Pixel Up! an international ux and design conference, and Source Up!, an international front-end web development conference.

Speaking and Workshops

I’ve been fortunate to speak for and with many people about the things that keep me professionally interested. If you’d like me to speak at a public or private event or on a podcast about design process, catch me on farai (at) pixelup.co.za.
