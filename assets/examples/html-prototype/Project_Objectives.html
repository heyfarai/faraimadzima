Project Objectives

Nedbank.co.za

For Nedbank, the new website should:

 

- deliver consolidated market-leading online experiences across clusters and platforms

- turn the website into a revenue generating channel

- reduce web hosting and product costs

- improve governance of web content internally

- create a perception of Nedbank as a provider of brilliant banking experiences

 

 

For Clients and Prospects, the new website should:

 

- make it easy to find and choose Nedbank products

- make it easy to buy Nedbank products

- make it easy to manage Nedbank products

- bring delight to everyday banking experiences
