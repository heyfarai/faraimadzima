What are Experience Principles?

 

Experience Principles give Nedbank and it's suppliers a common language for articulating and measuring whether something is or is not a Nedbank Experience. They help guide design decisions.

 

 

Nedbank's 10 Experience Design Principles

4. We engage not interrogate.

 

 

5. We use data to give clients value.
