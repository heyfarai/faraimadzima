What is a Persona?

 

Personas are user-centred design tools that allow us to make better design decisions based on what our clients need, rather than our own preferences.

Bheki Browser : Shops Around

"I don't know much about Nedbank,

what are they like?"

 

Key Verb: Browse

Deon Deliberator : Compares Products

"Should I choose the Nedbank product,

or this other one?"

 

Key Verb: Browse

Phumla Purchaser : Applies for a product

"I've decided to apply for a Nedbank home loan."

 

Key Verb: Apply

Sipho Seeker : Looks for specific product

"I know Nedbank has Savvy, but where is it on the website?"

 

Key Verb: Find

Neil New Online Client : Tries new things

"I've just opened an account, what can I do on the website."

 

Key Verb: Learn

Reggie Regular Online Client : Does routine stuff

"I've just opened an account, what can I do on the website."

 

Key Verb: Customise / Optimise
