I am a Nedbank Digital Experience

Nedbank.co.za

"I am a digital service that may look like a website or an app. But what I actually am is a vehicle designed to cater to people’s needs in a considerate and delightful way.

 

I represent the Nedbank brand, its values as well as its people. When I speak, I speak for them. When I act, I act on their behalf.

 

I take a full interest in people, their dreams and their goals. I engage with them conversationally, not interrogate them. When I speak, I speak their language. No geek speak or complex bank terms.

 

What I learn from people allows me to anticipate their needs and always be prepared to help them with that next step.

 

Around me, people can feel safe to say what they like and share their personal information, knowing they can trust me to keep it safe.

 

In my family we make it a point to remember everything unique about everyone. It helps us create a better experience, ultimately allowing the people we meet to feel comfortable. It also helps us make useful contributions to their lives.

 

I am an individual, but I belong to a family, we look similar and we share the same values and morals. It’s these values, not our software or hardware, that dictate how we behave.

 

I’ll say it: I want people to like me! I want them to tell their friends about how I dazzled them with my brilliance. When this happens, I know I am contributing to the Nedbank brand, its business and its values."
